# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Dashboard

## Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Internship

### Task 2: Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the Future Interns Data Science & Analytics Internship.

The objective of this project is to analyze customer churn and retention patterns using Power BI. The dashboard helps identify why customers leave, which customer groups are more likely to churn, and provides actionable insights to improve customer retention.

---

## Problem Statement

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges for subscription-based businesses. This project aims to answer questions such as:

- Which customers are more likely to churn?
- How does contract type affect churn?
- Which payment methods have the highest churn?
- What is the relationship between monthly charges and churn?
- How can businesses improve customer retention?

---

## Key KPIs

- Total Customers: **4,286**
- Active Customers: **2,720**
- Churn Customers: **1,566**
- Churn Rate: **36.54%**
- Average Tenure: **14.88 Months**

---

## Key Insights

- Month-to-month contract customers have the highest churn rate.
- Customers without Tech Support are more likely to leave.
- Electronic Check users contribute to the highest customer churn.
- Fiber Optic users generate high monthly revenue but also show higher churn.
- Long-term contract customers are more likely to stay with the company.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Encourage customers to switch to yearly or two-year contracts.
- Improve technical support services to increase customer satisfaction.
- Offer loyalty rewards for long-term customers.
- Provide special offers to high-risk customer segments.
- Improve customer engagement for customers using Electronic Check payment methods.
